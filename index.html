FAQ

GOT
QUESTIONS?

How long does a custom order take? +
Custom orders take 3–7 business days depending on complexity. Simple items (keychains, stands) are usually done in 2–3 days. We'll give you an exact timeline with your quote.
Can I get anime/fandom themed prints? +
ABSOLUTELY. We love printing anime merch — headbands, sword stands, figure bases, keychains, phone cases, diorama pieces. Submit your custom request and let's build it.
What file formats do you accept? +
We accept .STL and .OBJ for 3D files. We can also work from clear images, sketches, or a description. Simple items can be modelled by our team for a small fee.
What if my print arrives damaged? +
We've got you. Photo within 48 hours of receiving → free reprint + reship. No questions asked. 💯
Do you ship outside India? +
Currently pan-India only. International shipping coming soon. Email us at hello@streetsmart3d.in for special requests.
Do you offer bulk / wholesale pricing? +
Yes! 10+ units = 15–30% off. 50+ units = custom wholesale pricing. We've done merch runs, event giveaways, and corporate gifting.
Is 3D printing eco-friendly? +
Way more than traditional manufacturing! PLA+ is plant-based and biodegradable. We print on-demand (no waste inventory). Our packaging is minimal and recyclable. 3D printing produces up to 70% less waste than subtractive manufacturing.
Can I see a sample before ordering custom? +
For orders above ₹1500, we offer a printed prototype + photo approval before we print the full order. Prototype cost is refunded if you proceed. Just mention it in your custom order form.
